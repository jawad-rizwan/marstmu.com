About us


November 9, 2023

We are Mars, a student society that conducts various tutorials for tools such as OpenRocket and OnShape, with a primary focus on engineering and model rocketry. Our plan is to teach model rocketry teams, consisting of 1-4 members each, how to simulate, launch, and gather data of their flights. In the upcoming 2023 winter semester, these teams will be launching F or G class engines, where they will compete to determine which rocket performs the best. We have Aerodynamics, Ansys, Arduino and manufactuing tutorials coming up next.
